Three Cats Yarn

About

Mel is the Owner and Dyer behind Three Cats Yarn, a small independent supplier of hand dyed yarns based in Sydney Australia.

Mel prides herself on beautiful speckled and variegated yarns in a variety of colours reflecting nature and her surroundings.
